About Me

Here's What I Do

I am a web developer and Bootstrap specialist living in Orlando, Florida. I'm also the creator of a website called Start Bootstrap which is a library of open source themes and templates for the Bootstrap framework. I personally create and maintain all of the themes available on Start Bootstrap, and they are free to download and use for any purpose!

Iron Summit Media Strategies is the independent web development and digital marketing agency that I own and run. Our main focus is in front end web development for small to medium sized clients, and we also provide digital marketing consulting and services to a select group of local, nationwide, and international organizations.

Although I am a web developer by trade, my educational background is in business. I currently hold a Bachelor's Degree in Management (Entrepreneurship Track) from the University of Central Florida, along with a Master's in Business Administration (MBA) with a certificate in Technology Ventures.

When I am not building websites or helping businesses with their digital marketing efforts, I am either practicing Capoeira (a Brazilian martial art) or plaing some PC games.

If you have questions about Start Bootstrap, Iron Summit Media Strategies, or anything else, feel free to contact me and I will try to get back to you as soon as possible!
